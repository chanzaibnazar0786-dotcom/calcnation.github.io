Under FBR 2024-25 slabs: up to ₨600,000/year is tax-free. ₨600K–1.2M taxed at 5%. ₨1.2M–2.2M at 15%. ₨2.2M–3.2M at 25%. ₨3.2M–4.1M at 30%. Above ₨4.1M at 35%. Use our Salary Calculator tab for your exact monthly deduction.
